What We’re Building

KidDing connects parents and activity hosts on a single platform designed for simplicity, trust, and transparency.

Smart Discovery

  • Search by age, location, category & interest.
  • Personalized recommendations based on your child.
  • Verified hosts and transparent reviews.

Seamless Management

  • Unified calendar for all kids’ schedules.
  • Track enrolments, timings, reminders & receipts.
  • Safe communication between parents and hosts.

For Parents

Helping busy families give their children the best learning experiences.

  • Discover trusted activities nearby.
  • Filter by interest, age, timings & price.
  • Track all kids’ activities in one place.
  • Store receipts, notes & reminders.
