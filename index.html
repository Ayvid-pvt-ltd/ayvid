What we do

The problem

Parents struggle to discover safe, age-appropriate, and engaging activities for their kids. Information is scattered across WhatsApp groups, flyers, and social media, and bookings are often manual and unreliable.

On the other side, coaches, tutors, and activity hosts find it hard to reach the right families, manage schedules, and get paid on time.

Our solution

KidDing is a unified platform where:

  • Parents can discover, compare, and book kids' activities in a few taps.
  • Hosts can list activities, manage enrollments, and receive payments.
  • Data and AI help match each child with the right activities over time.

Target audience: parents of kids aged 2-16 and activity providers (coaches, academies, studios, community centers) in Mumbai & Bangalore initially, expanding globally.

How it works

For parents

  1. Sign up and add one or more child profiles.
  2. Discover curated activities based on age, interests, and location.
  3. Book and pay directly through the platform.
  4. Track schedules, attendance, and progress in your dashboard.

For hosts

  1. Create a host profile and list your activities.
  2. Set schedules, capacity, and pricing.
  3. Accept bookings and manage rosters.
  4. Get paid reliably and view performance insights.
